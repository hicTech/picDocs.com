We want you know:

  • -   This app was tested and approved from Dropbox team;
  • -   This app was tested and approved from Apple team;
  • -   When you use iDocs there is no communication with any server, all your documents are stored right on YOUR device (you can test this app works even if you switch off internet connection);
  • -   Only during the synchronization process the app will communicates with an external server and all your documents will be transferred inside YOUR Dropbox folder using a secure connection (https);
  • -   Your documents will be sent directly from YOUR device to YOUR Dropbox folder with no other servers in the middle;
  • -   During synchronization your personal datas (like your name, surname, phone number, email,...) will NOT be transferred or communicated in anyway;
  • -   The synchronization process doesn't involves other servers, this is a process between YOUR device and Dropbox servers. We don't actually know if or when you synchronize your device with your Dropbox folder;
  • -   To synchronize your documents the app will show you Dropbox login page. You can authenticate using your own Dropbox username and password. We DON'T store your Dropbox credentials, the app DON'T read these informations;
  • -   Once you have been logged in Dropbox the Dropbox servers will tell to the app that your logged in without transfer other informations;
  • -   The synchronization process DOESN'T remove any documents from your device;
  • -   The synchronization process has the only scope to duplicate your documents (pictures and name) like as they have been saved on your device. This version can't perform the reverse process (from Dropbox to device);
  • -   The intent of synchronization process is backup your documents ready to be imported in next iDocs version (picDocs) and shared on all your devices;
  • -   The synchronization process will save all your documents in YOUR Dropbox folder at the path: “Applications/iDocs2.0/ver1.2/your_iphone_name/”;
  • -   The app can NOT read, store, edit, remove or transfer the other files you have on your Dropbox account. The app can ONLY access the folder mentioned in previous paragraph;
  • -   If you manually remove your documents from Dropbox folder these will NOT be removed from your device and they will be restored on Dropbox at next synchronization process;
  • -   You should add, remove and edit your documents ONLY with the app;
  • -   ONLY your device can open your documents. Your documents in Dropbox folder are encrypted;
  • -   Your documents can be decrypted (visualized) ONLY from your device using iDocs. The cryptography key is stored JUST on your device and can't be read or transferred;
  • -   Anyway we recommend to manipulate your documents only with your app on your devices avoiding Dropbox folder manually usage.
